- `4`: strong
- `5`: excellent

## Blockers
Mark `yes` if any critical problem occurred.

- Pronunciation error that could teach the wrong pattern: yes / no
- Passage audio used before child decoding attempt: yes / no
- Child became overstimulated by audio: yes / no
- Child relied on replay instead of reading: yes / no
- Other critical issue: yes / no
